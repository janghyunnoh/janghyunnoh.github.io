## About Me

I'm Janghyun Noh, an M.S. student in Artificial Intelligence at Myongji University, advised by Prof. Minsik Oh in the Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics Laboratory (AIDA).

My research interests center on Bioinformatics, Medical AI, Deep Learning, and Single-cell RNA Sequencing Analysis.
